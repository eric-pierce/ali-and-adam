WE FIRST MET

We met on a cold Saturday Chicago afternoon in February at an Indiana University bar: Joe's on Weed Street. Indiana's Men's Basketball team was playing our biggest rival: Purdue.

Ali was with her good girl friends from college while Adam was with his guy friends celebrating his upcoming birthday. Our tables were next to each other, we bumped elbows, started talking, and just clicked. 10 days later, we went on our first date... and the rest is history!

The months leading up to our engagement were full of Ali thinking a proposal was close to happening, but Adam always throwing her off. As a result, Ali was completely caught off guard when Adam got down on one knee.
